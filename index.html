ABOUT COMPANY

QWAVE was established in 2015. As the long past management experienced successful in telecom businesses, such as trading of mobile base station devices, mobile base station antennas, fiber optic cables, feeders and accessories. Including repairs and maintenance services of electronics networking devices, advance antenna design (R&D) with own manufacturer for mobiles base station antenna systems. With long history on the telecom business, we enlarge our opportunity on information technology (IT) industry. In 2016, we focused on Networkand Security solution to launch the market with the variety of IT products in order to fulfill customer satisfactions such as NextGen Firewall, Internet Access Management, WAN Optimization and Hyper Converged Infrastructure and Infrastructure Server.

And now 2017 to 2020, we continue focusing on IT business and realize that, the era of energy efficiency and productivity in businesses and quality of life is coming. Inevitably, digitalized information technologies, will be key important foundation to fulfill advance application needed.

With our mission to be a Leader in Automation System Provider with Customized Solutions in Thailand by 2020, Q WAVE is committed to be a partner to fulfill customer needs. We, therefore, is to provide Internet of Things (IOT), Intelligent Building Automation Systems and Telecommunications to meet customers satisfactions.
